I'm Jae, an aspiring full-stack developer from Atlanta, GA. My interests include web app development, music, food, learning, and traveling. I am looking forward to seeing where all my hard work and passions will take me in the future.

< About Me >

I graduated from Georgia State University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am currently enrolled in Thinkful's Engineering Flex program to complement the knowledge I gained from my undergraduate studies with the technical skills necessary to become more competent as a full-stack developer. After I complete my program, I hope I can work on a team with great programmers and contribute as a valuable team member for my future employer.

I was drawn to full-stack development because it stimulates both my logical and creative sides of my brain. The logical side consisting of being able to break down a problem logically, and then coming up with an algorithm to solve the problem. And the creative side of once creating a program, coming up with ways of how I can bring it to life, and make it look nice and user-friendly for the end-user.

When I'm not busy coding, I'm usually enjoying movies, listening to music, checking out new restaurants, reading a good book, or visiting places I've never been to before to broaden my horizons.
